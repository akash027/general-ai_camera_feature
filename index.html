Your Requirements · Our Delivery

The 10 listed capabilities — and how we'd deliver each.

The scope below reflects the 10 focus areas shared by your team. Every capability runs 24/7 on your existing cameras and sends real-time alerts — with a short video clip — to the right supervisor via mobile app, WhatsApp, or SMS.

1. Shop Floor PPE Compliance

Detects any employee on the shop floor not wearing mandatory PPE — helmet, gloves, safety shoes, goggles. Instant alert with photo.

2. Idle Gathering Detection

Alerts when any employee stands or chats at a single location for more than 10 minutes. Helps reduce unproductive downtime.

3. Fire & Smoke Detection

Immediate alert the moment fire or smoke is visually detected anywhere in the plant — often earlier than conventional smoke sensors.

4. Walkway Monitoring

Keeps walkways clear and unobstructed at all times. Alerts if pallets, material, or equipment block a walking path.

5. Server Room Security

Alerts on any unauthorized person entering the server room. Video clip sent instantly to IT and security teams.

6. Gate Entry Compliance

No helmet, no entry for two-wheelers. No seatbelt, no entry for four-wheelers. Checked automatically at the main gate.

7. Vehicle Counting at Gate

Automatic count of loading and unloading vehicles entering and exiting the premises. Matches with dispatch and gate-pass records.

8. EOT Crane Safety Zone

Virtual safety boundary around the EOT crane operating area. Alert the moment any unauthorized person enters the danger zone.

9. Speed Limit Monitoring

Detects any vehicle exceeding the defined speed limit within the premises. Number plate capture available, subject to camera angle and resolution — confirmed during the site survey.

10. Employee Counting System

Real-time headcount of people inside the facility — useful for shift planning and emergency evacuation readiness. Name-level muster is available as an optional add-on.
